Use my testimony for courage perhaps, after eight years of an un-palattable and surreal defeat, humilation and recession I persisted sitting now at a horizon unto my dreams and a new life in Madison today²

  • Impact - 75 mph face-first into a stopped pickup & life restarted. Moderate grade, frontal lobe traumatic brain injury one of the worst. Two million dollars, and two years later we proved them wrong -

  • Year 5 - Five departed churches & SEVEN departed churches later I came to realize - she was right

  • Year 6 - Into a new home and stabilizing, my limp departs

  • Year 7 - My speech slur departs and my eyes go straight. Finding a new breath⁴ now, in Celebrate Recovery (in Madison too you may like)

  • Year 8 - Sub-Zero of Madison calls and things begin to make sense, the lights to life turn on for me here⁵
