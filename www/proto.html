Prototyping Feature - Mr. Troll

  • Software Wiki
"Lets build something cool for my portfolio", I said around last Halloween, and here a Mr. Troll head then began a looming front door greeting, feature-rich w/wireless control. Glowing in the dark over a pulsating laughter too, greeting you in ways unmistakingly horrifying check this out -



Summary
        @todo summary paragraph
        @todo handle left/right keyboard pic toggles

Features
  • Glow-in-the-dark head
  • Pulsing, glowing eyes
  • Spoken Greeting that is updatable by mobile app over Bluetooth
  • Video stream interface back to app, allows conversation w/visitors
  • Presence detection notifies new visitors with a greeting
  • And shares notification to owner by email over WiFi
  • Battery Powered
  • Can self-illuminate the glow in the dark
  • Rich UI for control & notification, mobile & web
  • ...?


Solution
  • Lets use Silicon Labs EFR32 for core CPU w/WiFi OB, a clean platform soln
  • Bringing WiFi & aux sensor detection over from Nordic nRF for crisp portfolio present
  • Low-power efficient operations are principle to design & demo
  • Have some fun, lets make this so no one can forget
  • And possible to build! Hosted to Thingiverse on completion
  • Starts with dev boards until (I) physical device assembled & (II) full feature set


Technology (Round I - Assembled & Established)
  • Core MCU - Silicon Labs Thunderboard (EFR32BG22)
  • Aux MCU - Adafruit ItsyBitsy (nRF52840)
  • Batt - 6600 mAh PkCell Lipo + USB Charger
  • Audio - Sound Board
  • LEDs/Spkr/Buttons as available
