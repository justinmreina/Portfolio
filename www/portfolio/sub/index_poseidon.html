Human Powered Submarine - Round One

Our entry to this idea was seeded by Adam Huxtable, and with a few colleagues and we began. Poseidon was a titan, 13' long and cylindrical in diameter there was plenty of room inside and fit well, which was important imagine transporting this behometh thousands of miles to a navy research base and hopping inside. Basic controls, scuba and mechanical were enough, and we transported Poseidon to Bethesda, MD for competition where she performed successfully (steadfast and slow!)
